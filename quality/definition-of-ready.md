# Definition of Ready

A PRD or ticket is "ready" to enter the sprint only when:

- [ ] Linked to a PRD with `verify-prd` passing
- [ ] Acceptance criteria written and reviewed
- [ ] Dependencies identified (no blocking unknowns)
- [ ] Estimate or appetite assigned
- [ ] Owner named (single R in the RACI)
- [ ] Success metric defined and instrumented (or instrumentation planned)
- [ ] Stakeholders informed
